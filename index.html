NimGamePlay

Rules in Nim

  • The game is set between two players.
  • Withdraw from the numberpile to reduce its total.
  • Each player has to withdraw between 1 and 4 units each turn.
  • Whoever puts the total to zero wins.

How to play

  • Enter your names into the field.
  • If you are only one player leave player 2 empty.
  • Choose an amount of marbles you want to be able to remove.
  • Use the numerical buttons to start withdrawing.
  • Good Luck!
